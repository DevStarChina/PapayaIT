Our Services

  • PROFESSIONAL SOLUTIONS
    Have a product idea? We work with you to turn those ideas into a software solution. Our product architects sit down with you to understand the domain and then help you determine the best way to achieve your vision in software. We can provide the guidance of what is economically feasible to implement. And then we implement, test the system, and deploy it for you.

  • COMMUNICATION
    Communication is a critical part of any project. We train our engineers and managers to be responsive to our customers, to keep you well-informed about the project and any issues. Because we are globally distributed, we understand that the most frustrating part of working with a partner is lack of communication. So we make sure you can always get in touch with someone who can help.

  • TRAINING
    We invest in training our engineers, to keep them up-to-date with the latest technologies and processes. We share best-practices between the teams, and where possible do cross-product training, to ensure that we have adequate depth to support our customers. we tend to keep engineers on projects for many years – this is incredibly valuable to customers because of the retained knowledge they carry.

  • Build Your Own Team
    We work as part of your team. We provide the infrastructure, human resources, admin support and personnel management functions, and then utilize a process that encourages your technical and project managers to engage with the engineers as an extension of your existing team. In this way, the day-to-day interactions between engineers take place as if the team was in the same building.

  • DEDICATION
    Our engineers are well-trained, coming from the best universities and enjoy their work. They share a sense of dedication to get the job done right. And they work with you to find the right solution for your needs. We create a culture that makes sure teams feel like they are part of our customer’s teams. Time and again they have gone the extra mile for our customers.

  • Application Management
    We deliver high-quality software with the speed and reliability that is crucial to a company’s success. Our goal is to provide our customers with a team of engineering talent that will ensure their time-to-market, quality and ROI for software development.
